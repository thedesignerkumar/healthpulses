about us

We at Health Pulses aspire to add “healthy rhythms” to everybody’s life.

Health Pulses covers all range of topics related to healthcare. Our views and advice are backed by exhaustive research and verification by our experienced team having extensive experience in healthcare domain.

We understand the problems of our readers to interpret the jargon and medical science terminologies, so considering that lot of care has been taken to present the information in more simple and understandable way.

At Health Pulses we would like to answer all your health related queries and provide update you on new research taking place in healthcare industry.

Our aim is to continuously improve on what we deliver, so we would be honored to get your feedback.
