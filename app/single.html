High protein diet is a healthy weight loss diet

by Health Pulses On July 24, 2016 0 Comment

Proper diet intake is very important for losing weight in a healthy way. The individuals who are trying to lose weight should also take high protein diet as it has been proved scientifically that high protein intake can help shedding not only “whole body” weight but also extra weight across your belly.

A new study which was sponsored by Dairy Farmers of Canada (a national organization of Canada) was published in the September 2011 issue of The Journal of Nutrition concluded following:

  • High protein diet coupled with high dairy products intake helps in losing belly weight effectively
  • High protein diets not only helps to lose weight, but also increase muscle mass. The increased muscle mass is very important as it not only changes the body composition which makes your body attractive and also prevents weight regain.
